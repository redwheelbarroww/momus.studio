The finding

The offer was interesting, but proof was the bottleneck.

Every cohort understood the basic promise: stress-test a funnel before spending more on ads. The hesitation showed up after that. Buyers wanted to know why this was different from a generic AI page review, whether the $750 beta price was justified, and what proof existed that the report would lead to useful action.
