Privacy

Simple data handling for beta audits.

This page is a plain-language operating note for the beta phase. It should be reviewed before scaling paid traffic or adding tracking pixels.

What we collect

For beta applications, Momus Studio collects contact details, business or agency name, website or funnel URL, campaign context, competitor URLs, and any notes the applicant chooses to share.

How we use it

We use submitted information to qualify the project, run the Synthetic Buyer Lab, prepare the report, and follow up about delivery or related services.

Client materials

Client materials are not used in public samples, ads, or case studies unless permission is explicit. Synthetic traces are labeled as synthetic and are not presented as real customer statements.

Tracking

The first static site does not require invasive tracking. If analytics or ad pixels are added, events should be limited to generic funnel actions such as page views, CTA clicks, form starts, and form submissions.
