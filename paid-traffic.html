Why it matters

Ads reveal funnel problems after you have already paid for the click.

A Synthetic Buyer Lab gives you a fast read before the budget ramps: what buyers understand, what they mistrust, what alternatives feel safer, and what would make the next click or application feel worthwhile.

Message mismatch

Where ad promise, landing-page headline, offer, and CTA do not feel aligned.

Proof gaps

Where buyers want examples, evidence, specificity, process clarity, or reassurance.

Competitor pull
