What Momus does

Run a simulated audience pre-mortem before launch, spend, or strategy gets expensive.

Before a decision gets expensive, Momus Studio runs controlled audience simulations around the thing you want to test, then turns likely objections, trust gaps, comparison dynamics, and unclear claims into practical next steps.

Code friction

Tag objections, proof gaps, message mismatch, unclear claims, and alternative pull.

Prioritize changes

Deliver the fixes, tests, and questions most likely to improve the decision.
