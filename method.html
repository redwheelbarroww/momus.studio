Method note

Synthetic buyer research, labeled clearly.

Momus Studio uses controlled scenario simulations to find likely friction, not to pretend synthetic traces are real customers.

01

Define the decision

We document the offer, buyer, traffic source, conversion goal, and competitors before any run.

02

Run buyer cohorts

Each cohort models a different decision posture: urgent, skeptical, careful, budget-sensitive, or overwhelmed.

03

Code recurring friction

Outputs are tagged for unclear offers, weak proof, price anxiety, missing process, competitor pull, and other patterns.

04

Separate findings from tests

The report distinguishes immediate fixes, tests worth running, and questions that require real customer or analytics data.

Boundaries

What this is, and what it is not.

Useful for

  • Finding unclear claims and missing proof
  • Comparing a page against competitors
  • Preparing A/B test ideas
  • Stress-testing campaign assumptions

Not a replacement for

  • Real analytics
  • Customer interviews
  • Sales-call review
  • Controlled conversion experiments
