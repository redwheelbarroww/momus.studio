Method

A faster read on how buyers interpret your funnel.

Momus Studio runs controlled buyer simulations across your page, offer, and competitors, then turns the patterns into practical fixes and test ideas.

01

Define the decision

We document the offer, buyer, traffic source, conversion goal, and competitors before any run.

02

Run buyer cohorts

Each cohort models a different decision posture: urgent, skeptical, careful, budget-sensitive, or overwhelmed.

03

Code recurring friction

Outputs are tagged for unclear offers, weak proof, price anxiety, missing process, competitor pull, and other patterns.

04

Separate findings from tests

The report distinguishes immediate fixes, tests worth running, and questions that require real customer or analytics data.

How to use it

Built for the messy moment before you spend more.

Best used when you need to

  • Find unclear claims before paid traffic scales
  • See how competitors may feel safer or clearer
  • Turn vague funnel concerns into specific tests
  • Give a team or client a sharper reason for the next change

Works especially well with

  • Landing pages that already get traffic
  • Campaigns about to launch
  • Competitor-heavy buying decisions
  • Teams deciding what to test first

Comparison

Where this fits among research options.

Generic AI review

Fast, but usually one viewpoint and light on evidence discipline.

CRO audit

Expert-led, but can blur opinion, best practice, and buyer interpretation.

User testing

Real people, but harder to recruit when the campaign needs a fast pre-launch read.

Synthetic Buyer Lab

Structured cohorts, saved traces, coded friction, and test-ready recommendations.
